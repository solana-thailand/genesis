## Flow of Funds
1.  **Incoming:** Stake from new members -> Main Vault.
2.  **Outgoing (Unstake):** Member completes Quest -> Transferred back from Main Vault.
3.  **Forfeit:** Member breaks rules or disappears -> Funds moved to Operations Fund.
