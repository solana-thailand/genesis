### Course Outline

The goal is to provide students with an overview of building high-performance systems and their application to international careers.

#### 1. Why Rust? The Tech Revolution
*Covers Agenda: Session Part 1 (Modern Systems & Rust)*
- **Memory Safety & Speed**: Why these are critical for AI and Blockchain.
- **Modern Systems**: How Rust is replacing legacy systems in performance-critical applications.

#### 2. Deep Dive: Solana Architecture
- **Parallel Processing**: Understanding how Solana achieves high throughput.
- **Developer Perspective**: A look into the network that enables low-cost, fast transactions.
- *Resource*: [Solana Executive Overview](https://www.helius.dev/blog/solana-executive-overview)

#### 3. Hands-on: Building & Learning
*Covers Agenda: Session Part 2 (Solana Playground)*
- **Solana Playground**: Quick start without local setup. [[Intro & Quick Start]](https://solana.com/docs/intro/quick-start)
- **Rust Learning Pathways**:
    - [The Rust Book](https://book.gist.rs/) - The gold standard.
    - [Blueshift](https://blueshift.gg/) - Interactive on-chain learning.

#### 4. Career Path & Global Opportunities
- **Community**:
    - [Solana Developer Thailand](https://www.facebook.com/groups/446906760534619)
    - [Solana in Thailang](https://www.facebook.com/SolanaThailang)

A discussion between **Katopz** and **Gui Bibeau** on the vision for Thailand's developer ecosystem and global competitiveness.
